Welcome to Brother Joseph Thamby Shrine

Br. Joseph Thamby

A Franciscan Tertiary, Servant of God

September 1883 to 15 January 1945

We are Franciscan Capuchins belonging to the Order of Capuchin Friars Minor. We belong the to Marymatha Capuchin Province of Andhra Pradesh – Telangana – Odisha in South India.
We are brothers and we live in fraternity; we pray individually and in common, we share meals and time together, helping each other to grow, as in a family....
